Welcome to the Simpson Garden Center!

We are a growing community on Simpson College's campus, and would love to interact with you! Our main hub is located near the Carver Science Building, but some of our members can be found outside of Kent and in Buxton Park oftentimes.

Our main building's hours are below. Please feel free to stop by for a chat if you're interested in botany and contributing to the flourishing natural environment around our campus. We would love to have you on our team!
