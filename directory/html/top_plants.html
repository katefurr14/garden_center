Our Top Picks

Here our two plant recommendations for your gardenscape this week.

Roses (Rosa)

Roses are a the flower of romance, love, and desire. They are very beautiful, and will be more meaningful to your loved ones that you grew it yourself. Spread love in the world with roses. They come in a variety of colors besides red and pink, so pick one that suits your garden.

Tomatoes (Solanum lycopersicum)

Tomatoes are a very simple vegetable to grow. They thrive the most outdoors rather than inside, and need to be very warm in order to start sprouting leaves. It is recommende to remove the bottom leaves from the plant to prevent possible fungus from spreading.
